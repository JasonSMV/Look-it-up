Welcome to Look it up!


What you can do...


Look unknown words up in multiple web-based dictionaries.

1. Highlight a term.
2. Either click on the extension's icon or with your keyword press "Ctrl + Shift + Y"
3. A popup windows will show up which you can interact with to: navigate between dictionaries, search new words, search on Forvo for pronunciation and search on Google Images.


Navigate between dictionaries.

Once you have the dictionaries popup opened, you can navigate between dictionaries.

You may use the button at the upper side of the windows to move either forward or backwards between dictionaries. Alternatively, you may use your keyword, "Ctrl + Shift + LeftArrow" and "Ctrl + Shift + rightArrow" to move forward and backwards respectively.

Furthermore, you may select the desired dictionary from the dropdown.


Look unknown words up dirrectly in the address bar.

1. On the address bar type the keyword "ns" that stands for "new search".
2. Type the word you'd like to look up.
3. Click on "Look it up" or press tab and enter.
4. Select from the list the dictionaries you'd like to seach the word.


Set your dictionary's preferences.

You may set your own preferences in the options menu. Access the options may by righ click on the extension's icon and click on "options" or click here. From there, you can do the following:
1. Active or deactivate dictionaries.
2. Organize the order in which dictionaries will show up.
3. Set your Forvo language preference.
4. Restore default settings.
